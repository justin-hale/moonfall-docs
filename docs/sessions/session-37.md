## Recap: The Fall of Helja Ironart

The session opened with players recapping the dramatic events of the previous session:

**The Hostage Rescue:** The party successfully saved most of the hostages from the great hall, including George (Olivia's nephew), Will, Roscoe, and Finnwick. Lady Corwin died in the battle, while several Iron Claw guards were dispatched by being pushed into lava or stabbed by the party. Helisanna's ravers joined the fight as planned, creating chaos among the Iron Claws.

**The Ultimate Betrayal:** Silas executed a masterful deception, pretending to betray the party by stabbing Bru and bringing a fake dragon orb to negotiate with Helja Ironart. While Silas negotiated and stalled for time, Bru (who had faked his death with a natural 20 performance) secretly planted explosives throughout the throne room.

**Helja's Death:** When Helja agreed to shake on a deal, Silas gave the signal. Bru's timed explosives detonated, causing massive structural damage. In the chaos, Silas attacked with a critical hit for 51 damage, whispering "What were their names?" to Heljaâ€”referring to his crew she killed in Greyport. She didn't remember them. Bru's cannon delivered the final blow, incinerating Helja from the inside. Her skeleton collapsed into her throne.

**The Escape:** The party fled through the collapsing throne room, dodging falling debris. Several Iron Claw leaders (including Grimjaw and Triple C/zombie Captain Corwin) escaped, while others like Car and Athena used teleportation rings to flee. Marta was killed. Ohma successfully stole Marta's teleportation ring with sleight of hand before kicking her off a ledge.

## Recovering the Ford F-150

After the battle, the party discovered that the teenagers (April Bonal, Bernardo, and their friends) had taken the Ford F-150 and robbed the dwarven vaults. The teens were using the truck to distribute gold to people along the road, throwing it at lunchtime crowds in a chaotic but well-intentioned Robin Hood operation.

**The Negotiation:**
- Olivia and Elspeth tracked down the teens at their campsite by a massive tree
- The teens were celebrating, having killed their "arch nemesis" and stolen massive amounts of gold
- A heated debate ensued about wealth distribution methods
- Olivia argued the teens were just "throwing gold at the same people" without proper tracking
- The teens accused the party of being out of touch with the world outside High Forge
- Tensions escalated until Leliana cast calm emotions on everyone
- Eventually, the party convinced the teens to work with Chalk Rock to organize proper relief distribution
- The F-150 would primarily become a government vehicle for Chalk Rock, though Taco Cat could still use it

**Key Quote:** "I'm not tryingâ€”I don't want you to give it to me. I want you to take it to Rock Chalk and work with him to find a good way to distribute it." - Elspeth

## The Epilogue: One Month Later

Christopher Hooper narrated an epilogue covering the weeks following Helja's death:

**High Forge Rebuilding:**
- High Forge was restructured under the leadership of Chalk Rock and a "softening" Grimjaw
- Hundreds of civilians had evacuated during the crisis, led by Chalk Rock
- Many refugees never returned, settling in new communities along the road
- The "cavern circle" training village became a bustling town overnight, renamed **Red Brook**
  - Red for the blood spilled (or dragon flames, or fallen heroes, or the new dawnâ€”depending who you ask)

**Lady Corwin's Mercenaries:**
- About a month later, Lady Corwin's mercenary army arrived, led by Captain Sir Eric
- They marched through, investigated, and left
- Official report: the city was "sacked and nothing worth saving"
- Soldiers who explored blocked-off areas told stories of "a witch that rides a black dragon in the bowels of the city" and "horrific heavy metal ringing through the halls" (Helisanna)

**The Vroomfall Operations:**
- The party used their Vroomfall as a mobile base and bubble of safety at the High Forge gates
- The F-150 became primarily Chalk Rock's government vehicle (much to Olivia's dismay, as he kept dinging it)

**Progress on Projects:**
- Bru's recording machine was recovered, with messages from Victor
- Bru, Olivia, and Scarlet made significant progress on the Stellaro (pocket village project), reaching about halfway complete
- Pepsi (the baby dragon) was successfully trained to be companionable, like a ranging dog that comes back when hungry
